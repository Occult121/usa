Stratosphere Tower

Ever wondered what it resembles to remain on the tallest perception tower in the United States? Well now you can! The Stratosphere Observation Deck offers unparalleled all around perspectives over Las Vegas and the valley. At 1,149 feet it's the tallest structure in Vegas and in all of west of the Mississippi River. Zoom to the top in the two fold decker lifts, going at rates of 1,800 feet for each moment (that is three stories per second!) and pop your ears en route! The famous Stratosphere Tower isn't to be missed and is a standout amongst the most significant things you'll do in all of Vegas. Ensure you pack your camera as you won't have any desire to overlook the shocking perspective.
